So, What is Streetwear?


Streetwear is a style of casual clothing that became global in the 1990s. It grew from New York hip hop fashion and Californian surf culture to encompass elements of sportswear, punk, skateboarding and Japanese street fashion. Eventually haute couture became an influence. It is the latest fashion trend among young generations.
